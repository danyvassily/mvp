Des vins d’exception, reflet authentique du terroir Gaillacois et de la richesse du Sud-Ouest

  • Le Perlé, vin blanc sec au perlant délicat, fraîcheur élégante idéale pour l’apéritif ou pour accompagner les fruits de mer.
  • Les blancs secs, allant du floral, fruité aux blancs plus complexes aux notes beurées apportées par la barrique.
  • Nos rosés de presse, allant du fruité et léger, parfait pour illuminer vos instants d’été au vin plus élaboré élevé en barriques.
  • Les rouges de l’AOC Gaillac, allant du fruité aux arômes épicés, aux vins plus structurés.
  • Les vins moelleux, allant des arômes gourmands de pêche et d’agrumes aux petits fruits frais.
  • La Méthode Ancestrale Gaillacoise, effervescence naturelle, parfaite pour célébrer vos événements avec authenticité et élégance.

An Enchanting Escape: Private Tours, Heritage & Sophisticated Tastings

During a guided 90-minute walk, be captivated by the timeless beauty of our traditional wine cellars, the serene grandeur of our French formal gardens, and the architectural elegance of the historic dovecote, a symbol of our estate.

Each moment is enriched by a carefully curated tasting session featuring our exceptional wines, showcasing the rich diversity of grape varieties meticulously cultivated to express the unique character of the Gaillac terroir. Perfect for wine lovers, families, friends, or groups seeking an exclusive experience.

Exceptional Wines – True Expressions of Gaillac Terroir and South-West French Heritage

  • Le Perlé Les Graviers, a dry white wine with delicate bubbles, offering elegant freshness ideal for aperitifs or seafood pairings.
  • Our dry white blends, combining Mauzac, Loin de l’Œil, and Sauvignon grapes, floral and citrus notes enhanced by barrel aging for roundness and complexity.
  • Claire de Lune, a barrel-aged Mauzac single-varietal, blending buttery finesse with toasted aromas to delight your palate.
  • Pressed rosés, light and fruity, crafted from Duras and Syrah, perfect for brightening your summer moments.
  • Gaillac red wines, from fresh fruity styles to spicy, structured cuvées like Rouge Tradition, Rouge Sélection, and the Pigeonnier, aged in new oak barrels for remarkable depth and length.
  • Sweet wines, including Poussin, a tribute to our family heritage, bursting with fresh peach and citrus notes.
  • The Ancestral Method sparkling wines, crafted exclusively from Mauzac, offering natural effervescence and authenticity, ideal for celebrations and elegant receptions.
