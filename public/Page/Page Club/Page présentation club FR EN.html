Château Lastours Club: The Exclusive Circle for Fine Wine Lovers

At Château Lastours, every bottle is an ode to passion, terroir, and craftsmanship passed down through generations.
Today, we invite you to join our Private Club — an exclusive circle reserved solely for our most passionate clients. More than a program, a rare experience — an immersion into a world where elegance, conviviality, and exclusivity harmoniously intertwine.

As a member of the Château Lastours Club, you do not just join, you enter a unique community united by a love for exceptional wine and refined taste.
Here, each member enjoys privileged access to the very essence of our terroir, within a warm and intimate atmosphere. A unique sensory journey where every encounter, every tasting, every moment is crafted as a privilege reserved for enthusiasts.

A world of exclusivity and tailored privileges

By joining this exceptional circle, you enter a universe where rare experiences multiply: access to confidential vintages, personalized storage in our historic cellars, invitations to private Château events, privileged meetings with our winemakers and cellar masters.

Each stage of this initiatory journey — Distinguished connoisseur, Emeritus Cellar Master, Ambassador, and Emeritus ambassador — opens the doors to an increasingly intimate and refined world, where luxury rhymes with the simplicity of authentic sharing.

Experience Château Lastours differently

Discover the intimacy of sun-drenched vineyards in the Southwest of France, enjoy exclusive food and wine pairings during starred dinners, explore behind the scenes with personalized visits.
The Château Lastours Club is more than a privilege: it’s a promise of shared emotions, a journey towards excellence and winemaking expertise, reserved for passionate connoisseurs seeking authenticity and discreet luxury.
